Who We Are

Building Dreams, Creating Leaders Since 2009

Excel Academy was founded with a vision to provide world-class coaching for competitive exams like JEE and NEET. Over the past 15 years, we have helped thousands of students achieve their dreams of studying at premier institutions like IITs and AIIMS. Our commitment to excellence, personalized attention, and proven teaching methodology have made us one of the most trusted names in competitive exam coaching.

Our Journey

1

2009 - Foundation

Excel Academy was established with a batch of 50 students and a vision to transform competitive exam preparation.

2

2012 - First IIT Selection

Our first student secured admission to IIT Bombay, marking the beginning of our success story.

3

2015 - Expansion

Expanded to a state-of-the-art facility with advanced classrooms and digital learning resources.

4

2020 - Digital Transformation

Launched hybrid learning model combining classroom and online education.

5

2024 - 500+ Selections

Achieved milestone of 500+ IIT/AIIMS selections, cementing our position as a premier coaching institute.
